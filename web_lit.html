WEB AESTHETICS:
Looking at Hypertext & Electronic Literature
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

What Is Hypertext?
Hypertext is text on a computer that can be styled and linked to other texts or with different kinds of media.
Other terms that fall under this category include hyperlinks, hypermedia, hypertext literature, etc.

Hypertext Mark-up Language - the basis of the web and web design.

What Is Hypertext literature?
Also known as hypertext fiction, hypertext lit is a form of electronic literature (or e-lit) that uses the web page
as a publishing platform. Making greater use of the web than the e-book, with the use of links and other forms
of interactive media, hypertext novels allow the reader to experience literature in a non-linear format.
