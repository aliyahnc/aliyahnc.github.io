Our mission

Formed in 1981 by parents and families in the local district with a vision, every year M&W provides a variety programs, services and recreational activities for families throughout the neighborhood, with the knowledge that strong community alliance fosters morale and wellness throughout the community.

Fostering morale & wellness means

  • Access to a variety of informational sessions and educational programs
  • Making childcare and health services available
  • Providing recreational activities for routine emotional care
  • Providing immigration services that keep families together
